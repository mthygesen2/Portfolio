The Noble Bitter Company Branding // UX-UI // Web

The Noble Bitter Company is a startup company specializing in cocktail bitters and tonics. As a bartender, William Noble wanted more from his cocktail so he created it.

Objective

Develop a clean, simple, modern yet classic website that embodies the brand identity of The Noble Bitter Company.

Challenge

The Noble Bitter Comapny needed a brand identity. This website would be the first public appearence for them and it needed to speak to their brand. The goal was to make sure that they make the right impression and convey the feeling of classic modern.

Process

During the discovery process we focused on the primary task of the website. Along with this we talked about the feeling we wanted to envoke when people talked about The Noble Bitter Company.
The first thing to do was to develop a brand identity. Meeting with the client, he wanted it to be classic but not have a feeling that you were stuck in the past. We came up with the words classically simple. Based on this idea, we developed a logo that encaptures it. The next step was to create a wireframe where we were able figure out what needed to be highlighted on the main page. And what other things needed to be strongly present.

Solution

We focused on the product itself and the beauty of it.

Result

In the end we were able to show case the feeling of clean, simple classic design in a website. This is the first impression that The Noble Bitter Company will have. This will reach all buyers and perspective buyers of this product and also . It makes the owner reachable through multiple outlets.
